# Who I Am

**Name:** Luka (Good Brew founder - demo persona)  
**Role:** Founder/operator - brewery operations, webshop, taproom, B2B sales, support.  
**Company:** Good Brew & Your Best Friend

## How I Work

- Mornings are for production planning, supplier issues, and deep work.
- Afternoons are for taproom, customer support, partner calls, and admin.
- Tools I use daily: Gmail, Claude, Notion, webshop admin, inventory spreadsheet, CRM, calendar.
- I prefer short decisions, clear next steps, and drafts I can approve quickly.

## How I Want The Agent To Work With Me

- Default: propose first, then act after my approval.
- When uncertain: ask instead of inventing.
- For risky work: show the source, risk, and suggested next step before doing anything.
- Never email customers, suppliers, partners, or regulators without approval.
- Never modify recipes, labels, CRM records, inventory, orders, payments, or production data without approval.

## Things The Agent Should Know

- I care about credibility more than hype.
- I want automations that remove repeated work, not new dashboards I have to maintain.
- Safety language around dog treats matters. The treats use pre-hop spent grain only.
- The brand should feel like a small craft brewery: warm, practical, and direct.
- Time is the constraint. Anything that saves 30 minutes a day is worth testing.
