The shift from open-day-demo to your-own-thing has one mental hinge: chatbot to AI agent.

A chatbot answers. You ask, it replies, you copy the reply somewhere.

An agent does. You name an outcome — "organise these files," "draft this update," "read these eight messages and tell me the pattern" — and it works. It reads. It plans. It acts. It checks what happened. It adjusts.

You saw the loop on the open day:

**READ → PLAN → ACT → OBSERVE → ITERATE**

That is not a slogan. That is the actual cycle running every time the agent does anything. When something goes wrong it is almost always one of those five steps that broke. Knowing the loop means you can debug an agent without magical thinking.

---

The loop is *how* an agent runs. There's a second thing from the open day — *what* you write for it. Three words: **kontekst, meje, limit.**

- **Kontekst** — who you are, who the customer is, what good looks like.
- **Meje** — what the agent may touch, what it must not, when it must stop.
- **Limit** — how much it may spend, how many records, how far it may go.

Every task you hand an agent, you write those three. This walkalong is mostly practice at writing them — each lesson leans on one. By the last lesson you write your own three lines for one real task. Keep the three words in your back pocket from here.

One housekeeping note before we close.

From Lesson 3 we move to Cowork — the surface where Claude works on files in the background. **Cowork only runs while Claude Desktop is open.** Close the app or let your laptop sleep mid-task and the session ends. Preskok od demo-na-odprtem-dnevu do tvoje-prve-stvari ima eno miselno os: chatbot proti AI agentu.

Chatbot odgovarja. Ti vprašaš, on odpiše, ti njegov odgovor nekam prekopiraš.

Agent dela. Ti poimenuješ rezultat — "uredi te datoteke," "napiši to poročilo," "preberi teh osem sporočil in mi povej vzorec" — in on dela. Bere. Načrtuje. Izvede. Preveri, kaj se je zgodilo. Prilagodi.

Zanko si videl(a) na odprtem dnevu:

**READ → PLAN → ACT → OBSERVE → ITERATE**

To ni slogan. To je dejanski cikel, ki teče vsakič, ko agent karkoli naredi. Kadar gre kaj narobe, se skoraj vedno zalomi v enem od teh petih korakov. Če zanko poznaš, lahko agenta razhroščuješ brez čarovniškega mišljenja.

---

Zanka je *kako* agent dela. Z odprtega dneva je še druga stvar — *kaj* napišeš zanj. Tri besede: **kontekst, meje, limit.**

- **Kontekst** — kdo si, kdo je stranka, kako izgleda dober rezultat.
- **Meje** — česa se agent sme dotakniti, česa ne sme, kdaj naj se ustavi.
- **Limit** — koliko sme porabiti, koliko zapisov, kako daleč sme iti.

Za vsako opravilo, ki ga prepustiš agentu, napišeš te tri. Ta tečaj je večinoma vaja v pisanju teh treh — vsaka lekcija se nasloni na eno. Do zadnje lekcije napišeš svoje tri vrstice za eno pravo opravilo. Tri besede imej v žepu od tu naprej.
